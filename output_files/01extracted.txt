## The Seed of Hope



## In the quiet village of Oakhaven, nestled between rolling hills and a whispering

## forest, lived a young girl named Elara. Unlike the other villagers who were

## farmers or weavers, Elara was a dreamer and a collector of seeds. Her pockets

## were always full of tiny, mysterious pods and kernels she found on her

## wanderings. Her greatest treasure was a single, iridescent seed given to her by

## a traveling sage. "This is the Seed of Hope," he had told her. "Plant it with care,

## and it will show you what this land truly needs."


## One year, a terrible silence fell over Oakhaven. The rains never came, the river

## shrank to a muddy stream, and the earth became hard and cracked. The

## villagers' hope faded as their crops failed. Remembering the seed, Elara knew it

## was time. She decided to plant it not in a protected garden, but on the highest,

## sun-scorched hill overlooking the village, where nothing else would grow.
