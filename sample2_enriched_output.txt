### **Option 1: Simple & Professional (Most Common)**

This is a direct, clear, and universally professional replacement.

### **Option 2: Modern & Approachable**

This version is professional yet slightly more conversational. It's great for networking, team introductions, and less formal business settings.

> **"I'm Shruti Oberoi."**

**Why it's better:** The contraction "I'm" is widely accepted in modern business communication. It feels friendly and confident without being overly casual.

### **Option 3: For Presentations or Public Speaking**

When addressing a group, it's effective to add a greeting.

> **"Hello, everyone. My name is Shruti Oberoi."**
